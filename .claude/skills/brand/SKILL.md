## Brand Overview

**Renaissance Park** is a private memorial park located at **Brgy. San Felipe, Tantangan, South Cotabato**, Philippines.

**Tagline:** Where Every Life is Celebrated
**Core promise:** Beautiful, accessible, and practical memorial solutions — a park designed for the living too, where families can visit, picnic, and spend time with their departed loved ones.
**Office:** Arellano Street, Koronadal City | 8AM–5PM Mon–Sat (no noon break) | 09225883675
**Park hours:** 6AM–10PM daily | Park hotline: 09272614681

---

## Services

- **Memorial Lots** — lawn-level burial plots in beautifully maintained park grounds
- **Chapel Services** — full-service funeral arrangements, 24/7 support
- **Transfer Services** — assistance transferring remains from public cemeteries
- **Pre-need Plans** — families can plan and purchase in advance to lock in pricing and reduce burden

---

## Customer Pain Points We Solve

| Pain Point | Our Answer |
|---|---|
| Hassle in transferring from public cemetery | We handle all paperwork, logistics, and coordination. Families just say yes — we do the rest. |
| Public cemetery is overcrowded and hard to maintain | Our park is serene, well-kept, and always accessible — no flooding, no crowding |
| Worried about the dignity of the burial site | Lawn-level lots, manicured grounds, and a peaceful environment that honors your loved one |
| Can't afford a private park | Flexible payment plans and spot cash pricing make it accessible to more families |
| No chapel nearby for the wake | We have on-site chapel facilities so families don't need to travel |
| Fear of being locked into a contract | Transparent pricing, clear terms, no hidden fees |
| "My loved one is already buried somewhere else" | Transfer assistance — we manage the entire exhumation and re-interment process |
| Feeling overwhelmed during grief | Our staff walks alongside every family — compassionate, not transactional |

---

## Unique Selling Points

1. **Full-service** — burial lot + chapel + transfer, all in one place
2. **24/7 chapel support** — families in grief don't work 9–5
3. **Park-style grounds** — not a traditional cemetery; a peaceful memorial park
4. **Trusted in Mindanao** — established, reputable, community presence
5. **Pre-need planning** — protect your family from financial and emotional burden later

---

## Brand Tone

- **Warm, not cold** — this is a human brand, not a transaction
- **Dignified, not morbid** — we celebrate life, not just mourn death
- **Trustworthy, not salesy** — families are vulnerable; speak to them with care
- **Aspirational** — a final resting place they'd be proud of

---

## Target Audience

- Adults 35–60, Mindanao, Philippines
- Families who have recently lost a loved one (urgent need)
- Families planning ahead (pre-need)
- OFW families who want peace of mind that parents are cared for
- Middle-income to upper-middle-income households
